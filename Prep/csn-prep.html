High-Tier Tech Package

  • STEM Spec Laptop: i7/i9 or Apple M-Series, 32GB RAM, 512GB+ SSD. Justify using advanced computational/modeling software requirements.
  • The Desktop Transformation: Universal laptop docking station, dual external 24"-27" monitors, wireless mechanical keyboard, and precision mouse.
  • Peripherals & Protection: All-in-One printer/scanner, recurring bookstore ink/paper allowance, heavy-duty laptop bag, and accidental damage plans.

Ergonomic Accommodations

  • Furniture Setup: Motorized sit-to-stand desk or fluid desk converter coupled with a fully customizable orthopedic chair.
  • Physical Relief: Dual gas-spring articulating monitor mounts (eye-level alignment) and an anti-fatigue standing mat.

Lecture Accessibility Stack

  • Capture Hardware: Dedicated high-fidelity digital voice recorder.
  • AI Note Synthesis Platform: Premium subscription (e.g., ChatGPT Plus) requested as an assistive study tool to ingest lecture audio/transcripts and generate structured, high-yield outlines and custom mock tests.
